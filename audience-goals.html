Audience and Goals

After conducting research, it is important to understand who the audience is and what the goals are for the Pickens County Humane Society.

Audience

To identify who the target audience is for the website, we wrote every possible viewer on sticky notes and organized them in a way to prioritize who the fans and possible skeptics are.

First, I wrote down every identifiable singular audiences and organized them in separate sticky notes. I created four separate sections and categroized them by who needs animals for therapy reasons, who's most likely to adopt pets (or fans), who is most likely to pitch in, and the skeptics.

The first section is categorized by the possible audience that needs animals for theraputic reasons. These people will visit the site in need of animal companions who are trained or can be trained as for personal service. The audience that fit into this category is the elderly, those with disabilities, and the homeless.

The next section is categorized as fans, or the possible audience that would adopt animals from a shelter besides buying from a breeder. These people will visit the site for the purpose of adopting a family pet or personal companion. The audience that fit into this category are families with children, couples, and college students.

This section is categorized as the possible audience that would use their resources to pitch in. This includes big corporations that are associated with animal care to donate to the facility in return of advertisement. This also would include schools where they would have classes or other volunteer related systems where students would come help at the shelter. Veternarians are also included in this audience because they could possibly volunteer to help aid animals or give supplies. Other sanctuaries or humane societies would also visit the site in search of supplies or volunteering in return for the same help.

The last section is categorized by the possible skeptic audience that would not necesarily agree with the idea of adopting from animal shelters. This would include other animal faclities that kill their held animals over time who may not agree with taking in donations or needing as much help as the no-kill shelter. Another possible skeptic would include animal abusers. We may not know who exaclty these peopel are but they are definitly out there so covering the imporance of the issue with animal abuse need to be identifiable on the re-designed website. Another skeptic would be those who do not agree with adopting from animal shelter for the soul purpose of only purchasing pure-breeds and/or from breeders.

Goals

    Overarching Goals

    Client
  • Recognition and awareness to get animals adopted.
  • Gain donation money to help fund the animals basic needs.
  • Donation of items for animals.
  • Getting individuals or groups to volunteer their time at the shelter.
  • Receiving sponsors for individual animals to fund or send items.
  • Reaching out to those who want to surrender their animals instead of “dropping” them.
  • Giving supplies and resources to people who have adopted from them (items, food, vet recommendations).
    Audience
  • Adopt animals from the shelter so more animals can be taken in.
  • Give donations to help the shelter fund the animals.
  • Giving items to the shelter (office supplies and pet supplies).
  • Connect with the shelter to volunteer.
  • Sponsor a specific animal to fund.
  • Give up animals that are unwanted.
  • Receiving supplies and resources from the shelter after adopting (items, food, vet recommendations).
    Website
  • Show the importance of adopting animals instead of buying from breeders.
  • Displaying the adoption application and making it easy to do digitally.
  • Make donations of money or items easy to access on the website.
  • Display animals that are ready to be adopted.
  • Connect with the audience to show animals “success stories”.
  • Showing that supplies and resources are available for people who have recently adopted animals. (items, food, vet recommendations).

Specific Goals

User Goals Content/Action
Adopters Adopt unwanted animals so that more can be taken in to then be cared for and adopted. Persuade them with the importance of adopting animals over buying from breeders.
Those who will donate, volunteer, or sponsor an animal Emphasize the importance of funding for pets since the shelter is non-profit. Informing them that volunteering is just as important as giving donations. Donate funds to the shelter to aid animals, volunteer in the shelter to help out with animals or office duties, and sponsoring a specific pet to fund their basic needs.
The community and larger corporations Spread awareness and educate the community around them about the importance of adopting. Increase adoptions, volunteering, and sponsors from individuals and bigger companies.
