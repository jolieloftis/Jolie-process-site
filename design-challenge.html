Design Challenge

What are we trying to solve?

What is the problem you're trying to solve? To help animals find homes.

Take a stab at framing it as a design question. How does the Pickens County Humane Society help animals find loving homes?

Now, state the ultimate impact you're trying to have. Recognition of the humane society to assist in the adoption of pets into loving homes.

What are some possible solutions to your problem? Designing a successfull website that is easy to use and get information on specific animals. Getting aid from donations with links displayed on the website.

Finally, write down some of the context and contraints that you're facing Recieving enough help and donations in a small town with not much advertisement or web attraction.

Does your original question need a tweak? How can the Pickens County Humane Society website be designed in a way that attracts viewers to gain attention to find loving homes?
