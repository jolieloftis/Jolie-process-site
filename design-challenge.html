Design Challenge

By answering questions from the Human Centered Design Toolkit, we can identify what our design challenge would be for the non-profit.

What is the problem you're trying to solve?

To help unwanted animals find forever loving homes by being adopted form the Pickens County Human Society

Take a stab at framing it as a design question.

How can the Pickens County Humane Society website help unwanted animals find loving homes?

Now, state the ultimate impact you're trying to have.

To gain recognition for the Pickens County Humane Society in assisting in finding candidates for adoption, donation, or sponsorship of pets to later be put into forever loving homes.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Designing a successfull website that is easy to use and recieve information on how to adopt, sponsor, and donate for animals in the Pickens County Humane Society and creating hierarchy in the website that correaltes with the goals of the Humane Society.

Write down some of the context and contraints that you're facing.

Recieving enough help and donations in a small town with not much advertisement or web attraction.

Does your original question need a tweak?

How can the Pickens County Humane Society website be designed in a way that attracts viewers to gain attention to aid them in donating, volunteering, sponsoring, and most importantly finding loving homes for unwanted animals?
